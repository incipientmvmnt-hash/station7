Littmann Classic III Stethoscope
~$110–$130
★★★★☆
"The workhorse. Not the best acoustics out there, but it's durable, reliable, and does 90% of what you need on a rig. If you lose it on a call, you're not crying."
  • Dual-sided tunable diaphragm works for adults & peds
  • Virtually indestructible — survives being stuffed in turnout pockets
  • Easy to clean, decent sound isolation in the field
  • Struggles with low-frequency heart murmurs vs Cardiology IV
  • Ear tips loosen over time — keep spares
